A Flappy Bird-inspired game featuring a horse character. Tap or press space to flap and navigate through pipes!

## Features

- **Classic Flappy Gameplay**: Simple one-button control
- **Smooth Physics**: Gravity and flap mechanics
- **Procedural Pipes**: Randomly generated obstacles
- **Score System**: Count how many pipes you pass
- **Pixel Art Style**: Retro graphics with Press Start 2P font
- **High Score Tracking**: Persistent high scores
- **Particle Effects**: Visual feedback on flap and score
- **Responsive Design**: Works on all devices

## Controls

**Desktop:**
- Space / W / Up Arrow - Flap
- Click - Flap

**Mobile:**
- Tap anywhere - Flap

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Click "PLAY" to start
3. Tap or press Space to flap
4. Navigate through the green pipes
5. Don't hit the pipes, ground, or ceiling!
6. Score points by passing through pipes

## Scoring

- +1 point for each pipe passed
- Try to beat your high score!

## Game Elements

- **Horse Character**: Animated GIF sprite with rotation
- **Green Pipes**: Obstacles with random gaps
- **Ground**: Bottom boundary
- **Particles**: Visual effects on flap and score

## Files Required

The game looks for assets in the parent `mario-horse` folder:
- `../mario-horse/horse-laugh.gif` - Player sprite
- `../mario-horse/thik_chhe.mp3` - Flap sound 1
- `../mario-horse/vishnuuuuuuu.mp3` - Flap sound 2
- `../mario-horse/aeeeeee.mp3` - Game over sound 1
- `../mario-horse/bapaooooo.mp3` - Game over sound 2

## Tips

- Tap gently for small movements
- Time your flaps to navigate tight gaps
- The horse rotates based on velocity
- Practice makes perfect!

## Deployment

Works on GitHub Pages with no build step. Upload all files maintaining the folder structure.
